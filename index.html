Vaccine Administered in Various States of USA

By Sivakiran Ayyagari - July 2021

The Corona Virus pandemic affected almost every person in this world. For more than year and half most people were confined to indoors, millions of lives were lost. But a hope came in December with the approval of vaccines. US was forefront in administering vaccines predominently Pfizer, Moderna and Johnson and Johnson.
